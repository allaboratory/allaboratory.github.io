About

The Archetypal Linguistics Lab (ALL) explores the cognitive, structural, and historical foundations of language through the lens of archetypal semantics, with a focus on the Armenian linguistic system and its connections with Sumerian, Akkadian, Luwian, Hurrian, and other ancient Near Eastern languages.

Method: Internal Reconstruction Focus: Archetypes of Form, Number, Order

Research Areas

  • Cognitive Semantics & Structural Inversion — systemic oppositions and phonosemantic mechanisms.
  • Armenian Etymology & Historical Linguistics — reassessing IE/Iranian hypotheses via internal reconstruction.
  • Archetypal Symbolism in Language — ancient signs and cosmological motifs (Šapattu, Sebittu, Ašxarh, Sapat).
